Welcome to the TWCS-RF Clinical Decision Support System

This system is designed to augment the diagnostic workflows of Medical Technologists and Clinicians. Currently, standard Laboratory Information Systems (LIS) rely on rigid, memoryless "delta-checks" that evaluate only two isolated data points. This prototype demonstrates the Temporally-Weighted, Cost-Sensitive Random Forest (TWCS-RF)—a machine learning architecture built to proactively track long-term biological momentum and alert healthcare providers to critical physiological shifts before they breach standard diagnostic thresholds.
